About Eider Elevators and Escalators

Eider Elevators and Escalators is a partnership firm based in Bangalore - one of the fastest growing cities in the world.

A bunch of passionate professionals with over a decade of specialized experience in elevators business came together to bring life to Eider Elevators, an organization that owes to meeting the highest standard every aspect of laying out and managing elevators. At 'Eider', we take pride in delivering the highest user safety, comfort, operational efficiency and overall customer delight experience Inspired by the flight, speed and stability of a bird - 'Eider', our mission is to deliver our very best at each and every facet of the elevator business.

With the right amalgamation of research and development, constant evolution in technology and respect for the relationship with you which we cherish the most, we promise you an experience that will be both meaningful and unparalleled. Eider offers complete elevator solutions for public, private and corporate sectors. Our equipment’s are suitable to installed in all category Apartments; Individual houses/ Bungalows; Villas; small, medium and large scale Industries; Hotels; Hospitals; Commercial complex and so on.

At Eider there’s a team of well qualified and dedicated people which includes management executives, highly qualified and well experienced engineers and very hardworking workforce involved in installation, maintenance and building customer relationship. We, at Eider, are eager to cope with new challenges as we see opportunities to apply our know-how and our wealth of experience in order to meet our customers’ needs and expectations.

So for we have completed 80+ elevators in and around Bangalore, apart from Bangalore we have completed the projects in Kolar, Bangarpet, Chikkathirupathi, Chinthamani and Thumakur. We are looking forward to spread our presence across the state this year. Our completed projects include Commercial complex, Apartments, Hotels, Hospitals, Showrooms (Royal Enfield, Jewellery outlets), Residential houses and Villas. Our key strength to differentiate with our competitors is our quality , we import most of major components from Germany with the collaboration of ‘Wittur Elevators components pvt ltd’.
